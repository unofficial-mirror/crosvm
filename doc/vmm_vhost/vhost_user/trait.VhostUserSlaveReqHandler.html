Services provided to the master by the slave with interior mutability.

The VhostUserSlaveReqHandler trait defines the services provided to the master by the slave. And the VhostUserSlaveReqHandlerMut trait is a helper mirroring VhostUserSlaveReqHandler, but without interior mutability. The vhost-user specification defines a master communication channel, by which masters could request services from slaves. The VhostUserSlaveReqHandler trait defines services provided by slaves, and it's used both on the master side and slave side.

  • on the master side, a stub forwarder implementing VhostUserSlaveReqHandler will proxy service requests to slaves.
  • on the slave side, the SlaveReqHandler will forward service requests to a handler implementing VhostUserSlaveReqHandler.

The VhostUserSlaveReqHandler trait is design with interior mutability to improve performance for multi-threading.
