Signal slave to enable or disable corresponding vring.

Slave must not pass data to/from the backend until ring is enabled by VHOST_USER_SET_VRING_ENABLE with parameter 1, or after it has been disabled by VHOST_USER_SET_VRING_ENABLE with parameter 0.
